Read Carefully

  1. The quiz has multiple choice questions.
  2. Each Question has 3 marks.
  3. Once you select option, then click on submit, then the result will be saved.
  4. if you select correct option but not submit it and press "Next" button , then it will be considered as wrong answer.
  5. On giving wrong answer, negative marking (i.e., 1) is included.
  6. You can skip a question by pressing the "Next" button to avoid negative marking.
  7. After skipping a question, you cannot come back to solve it.
  8. After submitting the last question, you can check your result.
